# CHECKPOINT — Men's Health Tracker

**Your body. Your data. Your call.**

A no-BS health tracker built for men. Track the stuff that matters, generate reports for your doctor, and actually understand what's going on with your body.

## What It Tracks

- **Daily Check-in** — Quick 15-second daily snapshot: overall feel, energy, pain, sleep quality, quick flags
- **Mood** — 10 mood states, energy levels, stress levels
- **Pain** — 18 body locations, 8 pain types, severity, functional limitation
- **Gut / GI** — Bristol stool scale, constipation days, straining, blood, pain, bloating
- **Sleep** — Quality, hours, issues including gasping/choking/snoring
- **Hydration** — Water intake in ounces, pee color hydration chart, urinary symptoms, nighttime waking
- **Headaches** — Severity, location, triggers, symptoms, prostrating attacks, duration
- **Medications** — Common meds + custom, time taken, dosage notes
- **Vitals** — Blood sugar, blood pressure, heart rate, weight
- **Sexual Health** — Erection firmness (EHS clinical scale), morning erections, libido, contributing factors

## Features

- Single HTML file — no build tools, no dependencies, no accounts
- All data stays on your device (localStorage)
- Color-coded entries: green = good, yellow = watch, red = concern
- Export to text file formatted for doctor visits
- Mobile-first design, works on any device
- Add to home screen for app-like experience
- Premium dark interface with carbon fiber aesthetic

## Usage

### Live Site
Visit: `https://[your-username].github.io/checkpoint/`

### Local
Just open `index.html` in any browser.

### Add to Phone Home Screen
1. Open the URL in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
2. Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Launch from your home screen like a native app

## For Doctors

Hit the **Export** button to generate a clean, organized text file of all logged entries — sorted by category with timestamps, severity scores, and notes. Bring it to your appointment or email it ahead of time.

## Privacy

Zero data collection. Zero accounts. Zero tracking. Everything lives in your browser's localStorage on your device. Nobody sees your data but you.
